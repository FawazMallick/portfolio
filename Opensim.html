OpenSim Simulation to Detect & Prevent Gait Perturbations

Concept

This project leverages OpenSim to detect muscles that are activated during slip recovery. The goal is to identify these recovery muscles and activate them using electrical stimulation in real-time. By simulating gait perturbations in OpenSim, we can pinpoint the specific muscle groups involved in regaining balance and develop algorithms to stimulate these muscles efficiently during slips or trips.
