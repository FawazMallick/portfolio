About Me: My Journey

My name is Fawaz Mallick, and I grew up in the vibrant city of Dubai, UAE, where I was surrounded by a blend of cultures and ideas. Currently, I am a Senior Biomedical Engineer studying in the United States at Rowan University. My journey into biomedical engineering began with a fascination for biology and technology and a desire to merge the two to solve real-world problems. Initially, I chose BME to combine my interests in healthcare and engineering, but it was during my studies that I discovered my passion for robotics. Working on projects like surgical robots and exoskeletons has allowed me to explore how robotics can revolutionize healthcare by enhancing precision and enabling life-changing rehabilitation. My interests lie in leveraging robotics to address complex challenges, automate processes, and create innovative solutions that improve lives. I am driven by the potential of robotics to transform healthcare, and I am excited to continue this journey of learning, innovation, and contribution to a better future.
